What the simulation tells you

A quick preview of the signals you will see once results are wired up.

  • Collation score: how evenly the checklist is distributed within each case.
  • Duplication risk: likelihood a buyer sees the same hit twice across a case.
  • Slot heatmap: which pack slots run hot or cold for certain rarities.

Quick setup cheatsheet

  • Start small: 4-8 pack slots, 2 rarity tiers, and a single chase card.
  • Increase complexity: add parallels and force one guaranteed hit per box.
  • Check variance: run 100+ cases to see if collation drifts as volume grows.
